Our Mission

Assisting individuals and their family members to deal with life's challenges. Strengthen family relationships and prevent family breakdown. Develop new benefit programs to meet the changing needs of young people and their families.
Increase social awareness and civic responsibility among young people. Collaborate with other organizations and professionals to build a caring community. Provide work challenges, training and self-development opportunities for co-workers.
