Physical Therapy Assistant Certificate

The 8th

Date:
January 5 to May 17, 2024, 15 lessons in total
Time:
2:00pm to 6:00pm
Location:
21st - 23rd Floor, Congregational Church Building, Causeway Bay
Charge:
$6,000

Persons aged 18 or above

The course is taught by registered physiotherapists in Hong Kong and is designed for those who intend to join the nursing service industry, engage in the nursing service industry, or are interested in physiotherapy. The course focuses on skills training for physical therapy assistants, covering work skills and theories in different homes and service units. Trainees will graduate with sufficient knowledge to assist physical therapists in their clinical work.

  • Introduction to Physical Therapy
  • Preface to anatomy
  • Physical therapy methods, instruments and tools
  • Use of instruments and tools
  • Posture and exposure
  • Exercise therapy—how to assist and guide patients to perform therapeutic exercises
  • Special patient care skills - transfer and assistance
  • Patient transport and assisting skills

Students who successfully complete the course with an attendance rate of 75% or above and pass the assessment will be issued a certificate and receive tuition reimbursement from the "Continuing Education Fund"

Tutor
Hong Kong registered physiotherapist, Master of Physiotherapy (kinesiology), graduated from the Department of Physiotherapy of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, and is a certified acupuncture physiotherapist and holds a diploma in physiotherapy and acupuncture.
